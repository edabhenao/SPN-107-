Lesson Plan: The Practical Power of Liberal Arts

Navigating the American Economy, Culture, and Future

Target Audience: Community College Students (First Year / Intro Courses)
Duration: 60 - 75 Minutes
Core Theme: Reclaiming the Liberal Arts as an economic and civic superpower.
Key Concept: "Human-centric skills in an automated world."

1. Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:

  • Deconstruct the historical meaning of "Liberal Arts" and differentiate it from modern misconceptions.
  • Analyze how the current U.S. economic landscape (including the rise of AI) increases the market value of soft skills.
  • Articulate how a Liberal Arts background equips citizens to navigate contemporary American cultural and political polarization.
